Divyanshu Vadehra

Will of Him

Cognized the injustices by the man, asked Him "Why?"

Heard the wails of the woman afar, asked Him "Why?"

Felt the weight of chain unwanted, asked Him "Why?"

Smiling, He showed

Him in Duryodhan

Him in Paanchali

Him in the arrow of the wicked

And Him in the bow of Arjun

Him in Kurukshetra

Kurukshetra in Him.
